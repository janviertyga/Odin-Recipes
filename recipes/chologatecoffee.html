Chocolate coffee

Chocolate coffee is a type of mocha drink consisting of strongly-brewed coffee, chocolate syrup, and milk. This Chocolate Coffee is full of rich chocolate flavor, so it’s the perfect go-to recipe for any coffee lover. Switch up your usual cup of hot coffee with this delicious mocha coffee drink! Chocolate coffee refers to a beverage that combines the flavors of chocolate and coffee. It typically involves adding chocolate to a cup of coffee or incorporating chocolate-flavored ingredients into the coffee preparation process. The result is a rich, indulgent drink with the combined taste of coffee and chocolate.

There are several ways to create chocolate coffee

  • Mocha: A classic example of chocolate coffee is the mocha. It is made by combining espresso or strong brewed coffee with steamed milk and chocolate syrup or melted chocolate. The chocolate adds sweetness and a rich, creamy texture to the coffee.
  • Hot Chocolate with Coffee: Another variation is to mix hot chocolate with a shot of espresso or strong coffee. This creates a delicious blend of chocolate and coffee flavors in a comforting, warm drink.
  • Chocolate Coffee Additives: Some people prefer to add chocolate-flavored additives, such as cocoa powder or chocolate shavings, directly into their brewed coffee. This method allows for customization based on personal preference, allowing you to adjust the chocolate intensity.
  • Flavored Coffee Beans: Another option is to use coffee beans that are infused with chocolate flavors during the roasting process. These beans provide a distinct chocolate undertone to the brewed coffee, resulting in a naturally infused chocolate coffee experience.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • chocolate syrup
  • sugar

Steps for preparing Chocolate-Coffee

  1. Add two tablespoons of coffee grounds to your cafetière
  2. Pour 125ml boiling water into your cafetière and place the lid on top
  3. Leave coffee to brew for four minutes
  4. Press the coffee once brewed and pour into your mug or glass
  5. Stir into coffee 1-2 tablespoons of cocoa powder until all dissolved
